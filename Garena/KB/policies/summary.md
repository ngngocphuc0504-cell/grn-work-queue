# 📜 Garena & Sea Group Policy and Compliance Summary

This document synthesizes and summarizes Garena and Sea Group's regulatory, behavioral, and technical compliance policies. It highlights critical **"Red Lines" (Ranh giới cấm)** and **GenAI usage guidelines** that must be strictly observed by Coby (Strategy & Operations - FC Online).

---

## 🔴 Part 1: Garena Red Lines (Ranh giới cấm)
*Violating these rules usually results in **immediate termination** or legal action (civil/criminal prosecution).*

### 1. Conflict of Interest & External Benefit (Phát sinh lợi ích ngoài)
*   **Prohibition:** Any form of investment, partnership, employment (full-time or part-time), or verbal agreement that yields personal benefit from:
    *   **Competitors:** Any entities involved in Game Publishing, Game Development, Streaming Platforms, Esports Organization, E-commerce, Digital Wallets, or Logistics.
    *   **Partners & Agencies:** Any vendor or agency collaborating with Garena.
*   **Scope:** Applies to all full-time/part-time employees and contractors.
*   **Action Required:** If you intend to engage in any outside activity or venture (e.g., investing in crypto/stocks is allowed, but running a side business or café should be disclosed to management to prevent conflict), proactively discuss it with the **Business Lead** or **Country Head** to evaluate risks.

### 2. Information Security & Personal Gain (Tiết lộ thông tin & Trục lợi cá nhân)
*   **Asset Exploitation:** Strictly forbidden to use company resources, systems, or authority for personal gain, market trading (e.g., selling in-game items on black markets), or giving unauthorized advantages to partners.
*   **Data Confidentiality:** Do not disclose confidential company info, including:
    *   Business development plans, product roadmaps, or partner agreements.
    *   Customer/player data.
    *   **Employee Identity Data:** Tên, số điện thoại, vị trí công tác... of other Garena employees.
*   **Cross-Referral Rule:** If you need to introduce colleagues to external contacts, do so via official channels (email) or with prior manager approval if using unofficial channels.
*   **Key Case Study:** A Garena employee in another region leaked patch notes and upgrade rates on Discord. This resulted in legal prosecution and massive damage to Garena's relationship with Nexon.

### 3. Island & Territory Sovereignty (Nhạy cảm chủ quyền biển đảo)
*   **Strict Neutrality:** No content, graphics, text, or features—in-game or external—may mention, imply, promote, or interact with sensitive political territory issues (e.g., the "nine-dash line" / "đường lưỡi bò", or claims regarding Hoàng Sa and Trường Sa).
*   **Approval Process:** If maps or political boundaries are absolutely necessary, they require **explicit written approval from the General Manager (Tổng Giám đốc)**.
*   **Key Case Study:** The game *Onmyoji* (Âm Dương Sư) was permanently shut down in Vietnam due to a map-related sovereignty incident.

### 4. Third-Party Payment Communication (Truyền thông Kênh thanh toán thứ 3)
*   **The Rule:** For mobile games, do **not** mention, link, show images of, or imply any third-party payment gateways (like `Napthe.vn` or scratch cards) within the app itself.
*   **In-app payments** must strictly pass through Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
*   **Approval Process:** Any communication or promotion of third-party payment methods (e.g., web-based napthe.vn promotions) must have **explicit written approval** from the General Manager and Regional Product Manager (Regional PM).

## 🤖 Part 2: Sea Group Generative AI (GenAI) Usage Policy
*GenAI capability is part of Garena's **Core Values** and performance evaluation, but security and privacy are paramount.*

### 1. The Golden Rule of Data Security
> [!CAUTION]
> **ABSOLUTELY NO PROPRIETARY DATA:** Do not upload internal company numbers, business plans, personal data of employees/users, or unreleased project code into public AI tools (like public ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

*   **Mock Data for Testing:** If testing a new AI tool, use mock/fictional data.
*   **Whitelisted Tools only:** Upload proprietary information *only* to whitelisted tools approved by Corporate IT. Check IT Center software licenses for updates.

### 2. 5 Core Principles of Responsible GenAI Use
1.  **Accountability (Trách nhiệm):** Users and managers are fully accountable for AI-generated output. One-off outputs must undergo human review before external distribution. Systematic AI processes must be reviewed upfront.
2.  **Security & Privacy (Bảo mật & Quyền riêng tư):** Prevent data breaches by checking compliance of the tools.
3.  **Compliance & Disclosure (Tuân thủ & Công khai):** Respect intellectual property (e.g., do not replicate voices, styles, or likenesses without consent). Disclose GenAI usage to audiences who do not expect it. Keep audit records of GenAI usage.
4.  **Accuracy & Bias (Chính xác & Khách quan):** Ensure outputs are accurate, non-misleading, and free from systematic bias.
5.  **Continuous Improvement (Cải tiến liên tục):** Regularly review AI workflows to keep pace with legal, technical, and business changes.

### 3. Prohibited vs. Recommended Uses
*   **Prohibited:**
    *   Generating deepfakes, malicious code, harmful/deceptive content.
    *   Bypassing procurement, access control, or LLM Gateway processes using personal paid accounts, unmanaged SaaS, or personal API keys.
*   **Recommended:**
    *   Drafting initial emails, reports, slides, mock-ups.
    *   Summarizing data, analyzing trends, extracting insights.
    *   Brainstorming, drafting code/debugging, and translation.

### 4. Integration & Legal Considerations
*   **Legal AI Review:** For external-facing applications, customer data ingestion, or API integrations, Legal must assess model terms, commercial rights, and regional compliance.
*   **Documenting AI Use:** Keep logs of foundation models, prompts, inputs, and outputs to support audits and protect potential company IP.

## 🤝 Part 3: Professional Conduct (Quy tắc Hành xử)

1.  **Personal Responsibility Online:** Be careful when posting, commenting, sharing, or reacting to content related to Garena or the gaming industry. Do not escalate disputes online. Report inaccuracies to the Business Lead.
2.  **No Unauthorized PR:** Do not speak to the press or act as a company spokesperson unless officially designated.
    *   *Standard Response:* "Cám ơn bạn đã báo nhé, tiếc là mình không thuộc bộ phận nắm rõ thông tin về trường hợp này nên hiện tại chưa thể bình luận gì về vụ việc." (Then report internally).
3.  **Respectful Frontlines:** Teams interacting with partners, players, KOLs, and customer support must remain calm, professional, and collect feedback constructively.
4.  **Influencer Disclosure:** Employees who are social media influencers must declare independence in their profiles: *"Mọi ý kiến/nội dung đăng tải/tương tác của kênh này đều là ý kiến của cá nhân tôi, không đại diện cho tổ chức tôi đang làm việc."*
