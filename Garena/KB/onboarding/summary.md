## 📝 6. Meeting AI Tool Protocol
When using AI transcription or summarizing tools in Garena meetings:

*   **Consent:** The host must notify participants and obtain agreement from **all attendees** prior to recording/transcribing.
*   **Prohibited Cases:** Do **not** record or transcribe meetings that discuss **highly sensitive/commercial secrets** or **legal matters**.
*   **Whitelisted Tools:** Use only Zoom AI Companion, ChatGPT Transcription, Gemini, and Notion AI.
*   **Sharing:** Share notes/transcripts **only** via SeaTalk or internal Google Drive. Do not use email. Limit access only to stakeholders.

## 📖 7. Core Business & Gaming Glossary

*   **A1 (Activeness 1 Day):** Daily Active Users (DAU) — unique users logging into the game in a day.
*   **A3 / A7 / A30:** Active users logging in at least once in the past 3 / 7 / 30 days.
*   **Activeness:** General player activity frequency.
*   **ARPPU (Average Revenue Per Paid User):**
    $$\text{ARPPU} = \frac{\text{Total Revenue}}{\text{Unique Paying Players}}$$
    Calculated over a specific time frame.
*   **AA (Active Areas):** Geographies with active player community clubs (used in Community operations).
*   **AoE (Area of Effect):** Vùng ảnh hưởng của chiêu thức/kỹ năng.
*   **A/B Testing:** Comparing two versions of a feature/event mechanic to optimize conversion rates.
