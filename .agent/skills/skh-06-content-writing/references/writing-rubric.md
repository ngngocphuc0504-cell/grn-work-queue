# Content Writing Rubric

## 1. Tone & Voice
- **Direct & Active:** "We increased sales by 50%" (Not: "An increase in sales of 50% was seen").
- **No Fluff:** Ban words: *delve, tapestry, crucial, paramount, seamless, revolutionary*.
- **Professional:** Confident, data-backed assertions.

## 2. Structure
- Every paragraph must have one controlling idea (Topic Sentence).
- No paragraph should exceed 5 sentences.
- Use bullet points for lists > 3 items.

## 3. SEO Constraints
- Include primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, and at least one H2.
- Semantic LSI keywords should be woven naturally.

## 4. Expertise Monetization (Solopreneur Lens)
- **Productizable Content:** Mọi bài viết phải có tiềm năng đóng gói (e.g., framework có thể tái chế thành template, course, hoặc lead magnet).
- **Outcome-Oriented:** Content không chỉ để "đọc" mà phải chốt hạ bằng kiến trúc hành động cụ thể hoặc một call-to-action chuyển đổi chuyên môn.
