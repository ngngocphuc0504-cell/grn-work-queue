# Workflow: Content Production (WF-10)

## Goal & Governance Context
**Purpose:** Transform outlines or raw data into highly polished written deliverables (blogs, posts, reports) using strict writing rubrics.
**Scope:** Execution SWARM phase under QUALITY-GATE.

- **👤 Owner:** `[@CONTENT-WRITER]`
- **🛠 Skill Target:** `[skh-06-content-writing]`

## Steps

### Step 1: Intake & Parse
- CONTENT-WRITER receives outline + DoD from QUALITY-GATE.
- Apply `skh-06` Intake frame to verify tone and audience.

### Step 2: Drafting
- Draft the content using M-E-C-E structure.
- Apply `skh-06` Tone & Voice rubric (No fluff, direct active voice).

### Step 3: Self-Review & Handoff
- Scan the output for banned words ("delve", "revolutionary", etc).
- Verify all DoD elements are present.
- Handoff output back to QUALITY-GATE for final DoD checklist.
