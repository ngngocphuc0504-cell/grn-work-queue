# Workflow: Slide Engineering (WF-11)

## Goal & Governance Context
**Purpose:** Transform complex textual or numerical reports into C-Level presentation structures with action titles and speaker notes.
**Scope:** Execution SWARM phase under QUALITY-GATE.

- **👤 Owner:** `[@SLIDE-MAKER]`
- **🛠 Skill Target:** `[skh-07-slide-production]`

## Steps

### Step 1: Storyline Blueprint
- SLIDE-MAKER receives raw report + presentation goal.
- Extract a logical storyline using the Pyramid Principle.

### Step 2: Slide Generation
- For each conceptual slide, generate:
  1. **Action Title:** 1 insight per slide.
  2. **Visual/Body:** Suggest chart type or max 6 bullets.
  3. **Speaker Notes:** "So What?" + Q&A triggers.

### Step 3: Executive Review & Handoff
- Verify against C-Level rubric constraints.
- Handoff to QUALITY-GATE for final assessment against DoD.
