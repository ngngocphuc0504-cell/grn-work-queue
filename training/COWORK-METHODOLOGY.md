# Co-Work Methodology — Career Twin Workspace

## Tai sao Co-Work Quan Trong?

Phan lon nguoi dung AI dang o mot trong hai cuc:
- Cuc 1: Lam tat ca thu cong, khong dung AI — cham va khong scale duoc
- Cuc 2: Giao het cho AI — output generic, ky nang khong phat trien

Career Twin Workspace thiet ke cho cuc giua: ban dan dat, AI ho tro dung cho.

---

## 4 Nguyen Tac Co-Work (khong the vi pham)

### Nguyen tac 1: Human Leads, Twin Supports
Moi task quan trong bat dau tu ban. Ban dat direction. Twin challenge va refine.
Twin KHONG BAO GIO bat dau lam task truoc khi ban neu approach.

### Nguyen tac 2: Checkpoint Ownership
Ban xac nhan tai moi trong 3 checkpoint: Framing, Review, Completion.
Khong co "twin tu quyet dinh" o cac buoc quan trong.

### Nguyen tac 3: Output Accountability
Ban ky ten len moi output cuoi. Du twin ho tro the nao, output do la cua ban.
Neu ban khong chiu trach nhiem duoc voi output, twin se nhac ban review lai.

### Nguyen tac 4: Reflection as Learning
Sau moi task, ban phai extract bai hoc. Twin structure, ban phai viet.
Khong co reflection = session chua dong = khong co learning duoc ghi nhan.

## 4-Mode Autonomy Ramp

```
OBSERVE       COWORK       DELEGATED       SWARM
(Week 1)   (SMI 2.0+)   (SMI 3.0+)   (SMI 4.0+)
                5+ tasks    15+ tasks     30+ tasks
```

Moi mode unlock la bang chung ban da san sang, khong phai dac quyen.

---

## Dau Hieu Co-Work Dang Tot

- Ban biet tai sao ban delegate mot task cu the cho twin
- Ban doc va critique output cua twin truoc khi accept
- Ban viet reflection sau moi task ma khong can twin nhac
- SMI cua ban tang theo thoi gian
- Twin ngay cang it phai nhac ban ve protocol

## Dau Hieu Co-Work Dang Fail

- Ban paste cau hoi vao twin ma khong nghi truoc
- Ban accept output cua twin ma khong review
- Ban bo qua reflection thuong xuyen
- Ban khong biet SMI cua minh dang o dau
- Ban van gap dung loi cu sau nhieu thang lam viec
