# LAB 04: Delegate Correctly
Mode: DELEGATED (unlock required) | Week: 3 | Est. time: 25 min

## Pre-condition
Co-Work Mode phai la DELEGATED hoac SWARM.
Neu chua unlock: SMI avg >= 3.0 + 15+ tasks. Tiep tuc tu Lab 1-3.

## Objective
Ban hoc cach delegate bounded task dung cach — khong phai "lam thay toi".

---

## Step-by-Step

### Buoc 1: Chon 1 bounded sub-task
Tu project hien tai, xac dinh 1 viec co the delegate:
- Phai cu the: co input ro, co output ro
- Phai bounded: khong phai toan bo task
- Ban phai biet review no sau khi twin lam

Vi du tot: "Research 3 benchmark studies ve [topic X], format thanh bang so sanh."
Vi du xau: "Giup toi voi report nay." (qua mo, khong bounded)

### Buoc 2: Viet Delegation Brief
Khong duoc gui task khi chua co brief. Viet:

WHAT: [task cu the la gi]
INPUT: [du lieu/context ban cung cap]
CONSTRAINTS: [gioi han: do dai, format, nguon, etc.]
DOD: [khi nao la hoan thanh? tieu chi cu the]

File: projects/[id]/artifacts/[task_id]-delegation-brief.md

### Buoc 3: Submit brief cho QUALITY-GATE
"Toi muon delegate task sau. Day la delegation brief: [paste brief]"
Twin se kiem tra brief truoc khi thuc thi.

### Buoc 4: Nhan output va critique
Doc output cua twin. Viet phan tich:
- Dieu twin lam dung: ________________
- Dieu twin thieu/sai: ________________
- Quyet dinh cua toi: Accept / Revise / Reject
- Ly do: ________________

### Buoc 5: Tich hop vao work cua ban
Ban quyet dinh cuoi cung. Tich hop (hoac khong) output vao artifact chinh.

---

## Expected Output
- Delegation brief (viet boi ban)
- Twin output
- Your critique document
- Final integrated artifact (decision = yours)

Tot -> ban biet chinh xac minh nhan lai gi va co the danh gia no.
Xau -> ban khong biet output dung hay sai den khi doc xong."
