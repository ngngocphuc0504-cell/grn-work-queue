# LAB 02: Frame It Yourself
Mode: COWORK | Week: 1-2 | Est. time: 20 min

## Objective
Ban tu viet brief cho task thu 2. Twin se challenge — ban phai defend va chinh.

---

## Step-by-Step

### Buoc 1: Chon task thu 2
Mo project moi: projects/[project_id_2]/
Hoac tiep tuc voi task khac trong project hien tai.
Submit: "Hom nay toi dang lam [task]. Approach cua toi la [X]."

### Buoc 2: Viet brief cua ban (khong xem goi y truoc)
Su dung cau truc SKH-01. Viet het 5 muc truoc khi twin respond.

Objective (outcome form): ________________
Stakeholders + DoD cua ho: ________________
3 Assumptions: (1)___ (2)___ (3)___
Scope in/out: ________________
DoD measurable: ________________

### Buoc 3: Submit cho twin challenge
Gui brief cho TASK-FRAMER. Twin se:
- Danh dau muc nao con yeu
- Dat toi thieu 1 cau hoi cham assumption cua ban
- Cho biet diem manh cua brief

### Buoc 4: Phan hoi challenge
Twin dat cau hoi ve assumption -> ban phai tra loi, khong phai twin tra loi.
Neu brief bi tra ve: chinh 1 muc, submit lai.

### Buoc 5: Xac nhan brief v2
Sau khi brief duoc chap thuan: "Toi xac nhan brief v2 nay."

- 1 insight: "Dieu toi thieu nhat trong brief cu la ___"
