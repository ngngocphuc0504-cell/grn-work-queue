# LAB 03: The Review Loop
Mode: COWORK | Week: 2 | Est. time: 30 min

## Objective
Ban trai nghiem vong lap: Draft -> Twin review -> Chinh sua -> Reflection.
Day la vong lap co ban cua moi phien lam viec.

---

## Step-by-Step

### Buoc 1: Hoan thanh 1 task artifact (tu lam truoc)
Lay task tu project hien tai. Viet draft hoan chinh truoc khi nho twin.
Khong duoc xin twin review giua chung — phai co full draft truoc.

File: projects/[id]/artifacts/[task_id]-draft.md

### Buoc 2: Tu danh gia truoc khi gui
Truoc khi gui cho twin, tu cham:
"Toi tu cham draft nay [X]/5 cho skill [SKH-0Y] vi ________________"
Viet ro rang — twin se so sanh voi diem tu cham cua ban.

### Buoc 3: Submit cho REVIEW-COACH
Gui: "Draft san sang. Toi tu cham [X]/5 cho [skill] vi [reason]."
Twin se:
- Doc artifact, chay rubric
- So sanh diem tu cham vs diem twin
- Tra lai feedback co artifact evidence
- Uu tien 1 dieu can sua

### Buoc 4: Doc feedback va chon hanh dong
Neu gap giua tu cham va twin-score > 1 diem: hieu tai sao co su khac biet.
Chon 1 dieu su ly. Chinh sua artifact. Luu version moi.

File: projects/[id]/artifacts/[task_id]-revised.md

### Buoc 5: Viet reflection
Sau khi chinh sua xong:
1. "Dieu toi lam tot nhat la... vi..."
2. "Dieu toi gap kho nhat la... bieu hien la..."
3. "Lan toi toi se thay doi... bang cach..."

---

## Expected Output
- Draft goc + revised artifact
- Feedback record: projects/[id]/feedback/[task_id]-review.md
- Reflection entry: projects/[id]/reflections/[session_id]-reflection.md

## Celebration
"Ban vua hoan thanh vong lap Review dau tien.
Diem tu cham cua ban: [X] | Diem twin: [Y] | Delta: [Z]
Khi delta nay < 0.5, ban da calibrate duoc tu danh gia cua minh."

## Skill Signals
SKH-02 (evidence_reasoning): scored from artifact content
SKH-05 (ai_cowork): +0.5 delta (reflection submitted, full loop complete)
